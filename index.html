üìñ Background & History

Amonto Girls' was founded in 2023 as a dream of implementing thousands of girl children in South Sudan. It is the only girls' boarding school with a conducive environment for learning. The name Amonto was carefully selected to reflect the identity and uniqueness of access to education for the rural girl-child as envisioned by Mr. Nobel.

Since its inception in 2023, Amonto has been jealously guarded and maintained under the great leadership of Mr. Arem Riak.

üìç Location

The school is located in Jebel Amianin along Juba-Nimule road. A quiet suburb accessed by an all-weather road with a beautiful surrounding which creates a relaxed atmosphere and a positive learning environment.

üìå Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission at Amonto Girls‚Äô High School.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have completed primary school with a satisfactory academic record.
  • Applicants should be female students.
  • Must provide a birth certificate and previous school records.
  • Commitment to the school's values of excellence and integrity.
