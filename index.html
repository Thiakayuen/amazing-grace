üìñ Background & History

Amonto Girls' was founded in 2023 as a dream of implementing thousands of girl children in South Sudan. It is the only girls' boarding school with a conducive environment for learning. The name Amonto was carefully selected to reflect the identity and uniqueness of access to education for the rural girl-child as envisioned by Mr. Nobel. The school is located in Jebel Amianin along Juba-Nimule road. A quiet suburb accessed by an all-weather road with a beautiful surrounding which creates a relaxed atmosphere and a positive learning environment. Since its inception in 2023, Amonto has been jealously guarded and maintained under its great management.

üí° Why Amonto Girls'?

The school has excellent resources and is at the forefront of technology initiatives with an extensive network system providing access to electronic resources. We place a very high focus on ensuring quality learning and providing opportunities for gifted girls. The school culture is one of high expectations, where students are safe to take intellectual risks within a very supportive and caring environment.

At Amonto Girls', we consistently educate our students to be female leaders in their families, communities, church, industries, and the world. Our girls collaborate, lead, mentor, and learn from each other. Not only do our students have abundant avenues for self-exploration and development, but they also form lifelong friendships and become part of a special community that lasts a lifetime.
